# Supported Sources

# Streaming Sources

Currently only a selection of video streaming hosts is supported by NineAnimator:

  • RapidVideo
  • Mp4Upload
  • Streamango
  • Kiwik
  • AUEngine of Anime Ultima
  • XStreamCDN
  • VidStreaming/VidCDN (unavailable for Google Cast)
  • MyCloud (unavailable for Google Cast)
  • F5 Beta a.k.a. PrettyFast.to (unavailable for Google Cast)
  • Tiwi.Kiwi (may not be available for Google Cast)
  • FastStream (unavailable for Google Cast)
  • OpenLoad (contributed by Awsomedudeopen in new window)
  • MixDrop (contributed by Awsomedudeopen in new window)
  • HydraX (and replay.watch)
  • Self-hosted Streaming Services (4animeopen in new window, Anime Twistopen in new window, MonosChinosopen in new window)
  • Facebook
  • OpenStream
  • YourUpload
  • Sendvid
  • Uqload
  • GoUnlimited
  • Streamtape
  • Nova
  • RapidCloud

For a more complete and updated list of supported video streaming hosts, visit our NativeParsersopen in new window

All of the sources above support downloading. More hosts may be added later. Feel free to open an issue to request additional hosts.
